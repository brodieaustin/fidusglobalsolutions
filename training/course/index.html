Students will learn

  • The history and methodology of Active Shooter incidents and o enders involved in them
  • To identify the mental and physical states that the human body goes through in times of high stress. Flight - Freeze - Fight
  • To identify steps to increase personal awareness, personal safety, and adaptability in high stress situations
  • To identify the various strategies of increasing one’s survival rate during an Active Shooter event
  • To display learned abilities during scenario based training and discussions

Logistics and Game Plan

  • Our staff comes to your location
  • All courses are principle-based for maximum flexibility in adapting to your facility and staff /students/congregation
  • Courses are customized based on schedule, course length, and number of students
  • All necessary training equipment is provided
  • We strive to keep student/instructor ratio low to provide a personable learning experience
  • All of our instructors are active or retired armed professionals with real world experience
  • We work with you, not for you, to ensure your needs are fulfilled
